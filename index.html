Special Chicken Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with tender chicken pieces and a blend of spices.

Experience the rich flavors of our special chicken biryani, made with the finest ingredients and cooked to perfection. Each bite is a delightful journey of taste and aroma.

Special Paneer Tikka

Grilled paneer marinated in a blend of spices and served with mint chutney.

Our special paneer tikka is a must-try for cheese lovers. The paneer is marinated in a rich mixture of yogurt and spices, then grilled to perfection. Each bite is a burst of flavor, making it a perfect starter or side dish.

Special Butter Chicken

Tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce with a blend of spices.

Our special butter chicken is a rich and flavorful dish that is sure to please. The chicken is marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt, then cooked in a creamy tomato sauce that is both savory and slightly sweet. Served with naan or rice, it's a comforting meal that you won't forget.

Special Veg Biryani

Aromatic basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and a blend of spices.

Our special veg biryani is a delightful dish for vegetarians and rice lovers alike. Made with fragrant basmati rice and a medley of fresh vegetables, it's cooked to perfection with a blend of spices that create a symphony of flavors in every bite.

Refreshing Raita

A cool and creamy yogurt-based side dish to complement your meal.

Our special raita is made with fresh yogurt, cucumber, mint, and a blend of spices. It's the perfect accompaniment to spicy dishes, providing a refreshing contrast that enhances the overall dining experience.

Delightful Sweet Treat

A classic dessert loved by everyone for its rich taste.

Enjoy our carefully prepared sweet treat, crafted to perfection with the finest ingredients. Perfect for ending your meal on a delicious note.
